How to Play

  1. Pick it: Select a difficulty level. Want to see the poses? Click "Show poses."
  2. Start it: The game will announce the first pose.
  3. Strike it: When only 5 seconds remain, a countdown begins â€” be ready by zero!
  4. Hold it: Stay in the pose until the next pose is called out.
  5. Flow it: If you miss a pose or the flyer touches the ground, you're out!

Modifiers: Some poses come with twists!

  • Reverse: Flyer faces the opposite way from normal.
  • Mono: Base uses only one arm or leg, not both.
  • Funky: A mono where the base uses the opposite arm or leg (mono foot to hand: base's right hand holds flyer's right foot, funky foot to hand: base's left hand holds flyer's right foot).
  • "Did you do both sides?": The game calls out left or right to keep you balanced.
